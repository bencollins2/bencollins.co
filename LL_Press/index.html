Lightning Love

is a band from Detroit Michigan.

They've toured alongside the Von Bondies, Tally Hall and Bad Veins, in addition to synchs in several films, including Will Ferrell's "The Virginity Hit."

Leah was recently nominated for Best Songwriter at the Detroit Music Awards, and Lightning Love has repeatedly been awarded Best Indie and Best Pop Band in Detroit by local publications. See recent press below.

They plan to release their second record, "The Blonde Album," later this year. But since you were kind enough to visit, why not take a listen to what they have so far. You can download a .zip file below.
